Gameplay

The Legend of Zelda is one of the most famous and successful action-adventure RPGs (role playing game) ever made. One of the the reasons the series is so beloved is the gameplay. In the game you wield many weapons, melee weapons such as swords and hammers, bows, magic, and shields. As the Hero of Time, Link is the one who wields the triforce of courage and the Master Sword. You Fight to destroy the wielder of the triforce of power Ganon, and to save the princess who posesses the triforce of wisdom from is his wretched grasp. You play in a mostly open world and advance the story by completing getting better gear and talking with the npcs, defeating bosses brings an incredible sense of acomplishment.
