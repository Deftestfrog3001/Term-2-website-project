Timeline

The timeline of The Legend of Zelda is quite a complicated one with multiple universes and no rules. Although there is an official timeline, no one knows where there newest installment in the series belongs. Many theorize that it is in a timeline so far removed that it doesn't matter what events/games preceeded it and is essentilly just tieing every game together and starting a whole new chain of universes. All this is because that The breath of the wild occurs thousands of years in the future from any other game.
